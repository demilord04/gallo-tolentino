About CICT Employee Management System

Our Mission

The College of Information and Communications Technology (CICT) Employee Management System is designed to streamline and enhance the management of employee information within our institution. Our mission is to provide a secure, efficient, and user-friendly platform for managing employee data while ensuring the highest standards of data privacy and security.

Features

Our system offers a comprehensive suite of features including:

  • Secure employee profile management
  • Real-time profile updates
  • Profile picture management
  • Emergency contact information
  • Department and position tracking

Security

We take data security seriously. Our system implements:

  • Secure password hashing
  • Session management
  • Input validation
  • XSS protection
  • Secure file handling
